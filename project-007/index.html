Why choose our tea?

Our tea is handmade by us for you, and we value our customers more than anything else. Our tea is fully vegan and gluten free, made from the best leaves in Dubai, has no added sugars or aromas.

We store our tea in the finest linen tea bags that are handmade by our factories in Yemen by the finest workers that are getting a fair pay, as we are all about fair trade.
